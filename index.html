About Me

The Artist Behind the Work

Passion meets precision in every project I undertake.

8+ Years

Transforming Ideas Into Visual Masterpieces

With over 8 years of experience in digital art and design, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and global brands to bring their visual identities to life. My journey began with a pencil and a sketchbook — today, I wield a full arsenal of digital tools to create art that captivates and communicates.

Every project I undertake is driven by a simple philosophy: great design is invisible. It doesn't scream for attention — it earns it through clarity, emotion, and purpose. Whether it's a logo, a character, or a complete brand system, I pour my creativity and technical expertise into every pixel.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Turnaround time depends on the project scope. Logo designs typically take 3-7 business days, while full brand identity projects may take 2-3 weeks. I'll always provide a timeline estimate before we begin.
The number of revisions depends on your chosen package. Starter includes 3 revisions, while Professional and Premium plans include unlimited revisions. My goal is to ensure you're 100% satisfied with the final result.
You'll receive your deliverables in multiple formats including AI, EPS, SVG (for web), PNG with transparent backgrounds, and PDF. Print-ready files are included with the Premium package. Source files (.ai, .psd) are provided as specified in your package.
Yes, rush delivery is available for an additional fee depending on the project scope. Please contact me with your deadline and I'll provide a quote for expedited service.
Simply fill out the contact form or send me an email with details about your project. We'll schedule a free consultation call to discuss your vision, goals, and budget. Once we agree on the scope, I'll send a proposal and we can start right away!
I want you to be completely satisfied. If for any reason you're not happy with the initial concepts, I offer a 50% refund before the revision phase begins. Once revisions are requested, the full project fee applies. Custom terms can be discussed for larger projects.
